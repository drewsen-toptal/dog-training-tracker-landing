How to Train "Stay"

1

Have Max sit first. This is the foundation for Stay.

2

Hold your palm up facing Max and say "Stay" in a firm voice.

3

Take one step back. Wait 2-3 seconds.

4

If Max stays, click and reward! Return and give treats.

5

Gradually increase distance and duration as Max improves.

Pro Tips

Always release Max with a consistent word like "Okay!" or "Free!"

Start with short stays (2-3 seconds) and build up slowly

Practice in low-distraction areas first before adding challenges
